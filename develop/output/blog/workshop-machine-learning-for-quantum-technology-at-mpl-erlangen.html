Workshop Machine Learning for Quantum Technology at MPL Erlangen

Posted in misc on 29-01-2019 by PhysicsML

The Max Planck Institute for the Science of Light in Erlangen, Germany, hosts "Machine Learning for Quantum Technology" on May 8-10 (2019) - an international workshop that covers emerging applications of machine learning to quantum devices, including advanced methods like reinforcement learning.

The workshop will be preceded by a two-day school for students trying to enter the field, going from a recap of the basics of neural networks to advanced techniques, May 6-7, 2019.

List of invited speakers and more details can be found on the workshop website.
